Welcome to E.M Academy

E.M at a glance

Established in 1948, E.M academy, E.M as it is fondly referred to, is the first Academy in Nigeria. Until 1962 when it became a full-fledged independent academy, it was a College in a special relationship scheme. The academy, which took off with academic programmes in Arts, Science and Medicine, is now a comprehensive citadel of learning with academic programmes in sixteen Faculties namely, Arts, Science, Basic Medical Sciences, Clinical Sciences, Agriculture, the Social Sciences, Education, Veterinary Medicine, Pharmacy, Technology, Law, Public Health, Dentistry, Economics, Renewable Natural Resources and Environmental Design and Management.
